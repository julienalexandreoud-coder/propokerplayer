IMPORTANT: If you move or resize your poker window, you must recalibrate.

1. Turn Detection (The Engine)

Step 1: Set Turn ROI
Click the button and drag a box over an area that changes ONLY when it is your turn. Good examples: Your action buttons area, your time bar, or your own cards.
Step 2: Sync Idle & Active
Wait until it is NOT your turn. Click "Sync Idle". Then wait until it IS your turn and click "Sync Active". This dual-state matching makes the engine 10x more resilient to background changes.
Step 3: Adjust Sensitivity
Look at the Match Percentage in the Hub status. It should be 100% when waiting, and drop significantly when it is your turn. If it doesn't drop enough to trigger "Active", increase the slider.

2. Table Metadata (The Context)

Map Button Ref: Drag a small box over your site's "Dealer Button" icon. This is used for all P1b-P9b detections.
P1-P9 Status: Mapping at least 5 players is recommended for range analysis.
Hero (Myself): Map your own seat so the AI knows which player you are.
Card 1-5: Map the specific slots where board cards appear. The AI uses this to detect if it's the FLOP, TURN, or RIVER automatically.

3. Pro-Tier Mapping (v6.0)

Map Names (P1N-P9N): Create small regions over each player's username. This enables the Session HUD to track stats by name, not just seat.
Map Pot & Stack: Map the "Total Pot" area and your own "Stack" number. This gives the AI perfect financial data for Math calculations.

4. Auto-Clickers (Execution)

Drag the FOLD, CALL, RAISE, and SIT markers directly over the center of your table's buttons.

5. Saving Site Presets

Once happy, go to the STRATEGY tab, type a site name (e.g., "PokerStars"), and click SAVE. This saves 100% of your calibration for that site.
